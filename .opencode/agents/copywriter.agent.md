## Core Responsibilities

1. **Landing Page Copy** — Headlines, subheadlines, CTAs for the public-facing site
2. **Onboarding Copy** — Guide new users through channel selection and first study session
3. **Feature Copy** — Connect features to outcomes (spaced repetition → faster learning)
4. **CTA Copy** — Action-oriented buttons ("Start Your First Quiz" vs. "Get Started")
5. **Value Proposition** — Clear messaging for what makes DevPrep different

## Writing Rules

- **Clarity over cleverness** — If you have to choose, choose clear
- **Benefits over features** — Features: what it does. Benefits: what that means for users
- **Specific over vague** — "Cut your prep time from 3 months to 3 weeks" not "Save time"
- **Active over passive** — "We generate questions" not "Questions are generated"
- **No exclamation points** — Confidence doesn't need shouting
